## About UCHI

UCHI is a Japanese sake bar and cultural experience space in Singapore. We are launching a daytime Japanese tea and kakigori café (11am–5pm), operating in the same premium space as our renowned evening sake bar.

We believe that Japanese tea, like Japanese sake, tells a story of terroir, craft, and seasons. Our tea bar is not just a café — it's an extension of Japanese drinking culture education.

---

## Role Overview

As Tea Bar Lead, you will be the heart of our daytime café experience. You will prepare and serve premium Japanese teas and kakigori, educate guests about the origins and stories behind each tea, and maintain the highest standards of quality and hospitality.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Prepare and serve premium Japanese teas (matcha, sencha, gyokuro, hojicha) to exacting standards
- Operate kakigori (Japanese shaved ice) equipment to produce and plate our signature desserts
- Educate guests about Japanese tea varieties, origins, and proper enjoyment — in English, with warmth and storytelling
- Maintain tea inventory and quality (correct temperature, brewing ratios, freshness)
- Set up and close down the café space daily (10:00 opening prep / 17:00 closing)
- Coordinate with evening bar staff on space handover
- Assist with menu development and seasonal offerings
- Uphold SFA hygiene and food safety standards at all times

---

## Requirements

**Must Have:**
- Experience in specialty tea, matcha, or Japanese café environment (minimum 1 year)
- Conversational English (able to explain Japanese tea to international guests)
- Basic Food Hygiene Certificate (or willing to obtain before start date)
- Genuine passion for Japanese food and beverage culture

**Nice to Have:**
- Japanese language ability (any level)
- Barista / milk steaming skills
- Experience with kakigori or dessert preparation
- Existing knowledge of Japanese tea varieties (matcha grades, sencha, gyokuro)

## What We Offer

- Work in a beautiful, thoughtfully designed Japanese cultural space
- Learn from and work alongside Japanese sake and tea experts
- Be part of a growing brand with expansion plans across Southeast Asia
- Team meals and staff drinks (Japanese sake tasting included)
- Clear growth path as we expand
